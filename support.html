Frequently asked questions

How does Nuggets work?

Nuggets is a daily 5-minute learning game. Each day you'll get a "nugget" — one big idea from a top self-help book — followed by quick interactive games that help you apply and remember it.

Is Nuggets free?

You can try Nuggets for free. Some features and content packs are available with a subscription. You can manage your subscription from your device's App Store or Google Play account settings.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Nuggets → Profile → Manage Subscription → Restore Purchases. If you continue to have trouble, email swsri@pm.me with your store receipt.

How do I cancel my subscription?

iOS: Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Nuggets → Cancel.
Android: Open Google Play → tap your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Nuggets → Cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Open Nuggets → Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated learning data within 30 days. Alternatively, email swsri@pm.me from your account email and we'll process it for you.

I lost my streak — can you restore it?

Email us at swsri@pm.me with the date you missed and we'll do our best to help.

I have a bug to report or a feature to suggest

We love feedback. Email swsri@pm.me with your device model, OS version, and a short description (screenshots help!).
