Delete your account

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Nuggets lets you permanently delete your account and the data associated with it. You can do this directly inside the app in a few taps, or send us an email if you no longer have the app installed.

1. Delete from inside the app

  1. Open Nuggets and sign in if prompted.
  2. Tap the You tab in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the profile screen.
  4. Tap Delete account (shown beneath Sign out).
  5. Confirm the destructive action in the dialog that appears.

The app calls our server immediately to remove your account. You are returned to the sign-in screen once deletion completes.

2. Delete by email request

If you no longer have access to the app, send an email from the address associated with your Nuggets account to swsri@pm.me with the subject "Delete my Nuggets account". We respond within 30 days and delete the account once we can verify the request is coming from the account holder.

3. What gets deleted

The following data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered:

  • Your account record and authentication credentials
  • Selected learning areas and primary-area choice
  • Concept progress, mastery levels, and spaced-repetition state
  • Session completion history, earned XP, and streak data
  • Apply-It implementation intentions and their resolution outcomes
  • Sign-in provider links (Google / Apple)
  • Device push-notification tokens

4. What is retained, and for how long

A small amount of data is retained only as required for legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, or security purposes:

  • Subscription and billing records — up to 7 years, to meet tax, accounting, and consumer-protection requirements. These records do not contain learning content or progress.
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics already detached from any account identifier may persist in aggregate form.
  • Server-side logs are automatically rotated and purged within 90 days.

5. Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription. Cancel separately in the Google Play Store (Subscriptions → Nuggets) or the Apple App Store (Settings → your name → Subscriptions) before deleting the account to stop further billing.
